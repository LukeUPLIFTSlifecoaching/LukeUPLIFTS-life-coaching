About Me

Hi, I'm Luke, the founder of Luke's Life Coaching, based in Cambridge, UK. Everyone has a story, and mine is one of profound transformation from the depths of despair to unbreakable confidence and purpose. As a coach, I have a firm belief in the greater good—that there are no bad people in this world, only good souls burdened by mixed or misguided belief systems, shaped by high levels of trauma and distress. I reject failure and stereotypes entirely; every individual is uniquely amazing, a dreamer with infinite potential.

My journey took me to the lowest points: battling ADHD, the grief of losing my dad at just 2 years old, relentless bullying, addiction, and a lifetime of feeling misunderstood, pushed aside, lost, and alone in a world of billions. I was trapped in the relentless buzz of my own mind, silenced by the noise of others who didn't truly know me. One day, I woke up and declared enough was enough. I knew this shell of a person wasn't the real me, so I invested in myself—getting motivated in the gym, learning new skills, and finding faith in God. These steps allowed the scared little boy inside to emerge and truly live. They built my confidence, taught me to embrace solitude with genuine happiness, and helped me process and release the grief of losing my dad.

This rebirth ignited my passion for mentoring, sparking a deep drive to help others overcome life's daily struggles, like mental health challenges, depression, anxiety, and the impacts I've seen affect so many lives—including my own. It led me to a profound self-belief: I am confident, smart, and destined to become everything I dream of, because I am enough—a dreamer and a doer. No dream is too big; you just have to grow to smash it. We're all here to learn, evolve, and achieve anything we set our minds to. That's why I started Luke's Life Coaching: to guide others out of their pain, silence the inner chaos, heal deep wounds, foster unshakeable self-assurance, find joy in being alone, and pursue those bold dreams with grit and growth.

Through my personalized 1-to-1 coaching sessions, held in luxurious venues to create an atmosphere of class and professionalism that inspires excellence, and my empowering 1-to-many group programs, I help you turn obstacles into triumphs. I believe in a structured approach that breaks the journey down into achievable steps—much like a 4-step program—to build lasting change, with a focus on future goals rather than past struggles. Using techniques like motivational interviewing, I empower you in a genuine, empathetic style that makes sessions feel like a conversation with a friend. As I continue building my credentials through ongoing learning and expert feedback, I'm committed to your unique path. Whether you're navigating trauma, ADHD, loss, or everyday challenges, let's work together to create the life you deserve—let's grow, thrive, and unlock the amazing person you were always meant to be!
